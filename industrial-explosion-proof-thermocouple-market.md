<p><p>The industrial explosion-proof thermocouple market features two main types: wire-wound RTD sensors and thin film RTD sensors. Wire-wound RTD sensors consist of a coiled metal wire that provides high accuracy and stability in temperature measurement, suitable for harsh environments. In contrast, thin film RTD sensors utilize a thin layer of metal deposited on a ceramic substrate, offering faster response times and compact designs. Both types ensure safety and reliability in explosive atmospheres, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
