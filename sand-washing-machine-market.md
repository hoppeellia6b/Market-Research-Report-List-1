<p><ul><li>Wheel Sand Washing Machine</li><li>Screw Sand Washing Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sand washing machine market comprises primarily two types: wheel sand washing machines and screw sand washing machines. Wheel sand washing machines utilize a rotating wheel to agitate sand and water, effectively removing impurities and producing clean sand. They are efficient and suitable for various applications. Screw sand washing machines, on the other hand, use a helical screw for washing and dewatering sand, providing a continuous operation with high dirt separation efficiency. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, enhancing sand quality.</p></p>
