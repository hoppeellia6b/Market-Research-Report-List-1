<p><p>The benchtop automated cell sorter market includes various types tailored for specific sorting needs. Single Cell Sorters isolate individual cells, ideal for single-cell analysis. Fluorescent Activated Cell Sorters (FACS) utilize fluorescent markers to identify and sort cells based on specific characteristics. Magnetic Cell Sorters leverage magnetic nanoparticles for targeted separation. Buoyancy Activated Cell Sorters focus on the density differences of cells to facilitate sorting. Each type serves distinct applications in research, diagnostics, and therapeutic development.</p></p>
