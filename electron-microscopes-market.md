<p><strong>The Electron Microscopes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Scanning Electron Microscopes (SEMs)</li><li>Transmission Electron Microscopes (TEMs)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electron microscope market is primarily categorized into two types: Scanning Electron Microscopes (SEMs) and Transmission Electron Microscopes (TEMs). SEMs provide detailed surface topography and composition by scanning samples with focused electron beams, making them ideal for materials science and biology. In contrast, TEMs offer high-resolution imaging by transmitting electrons through ultra-thin samples, allowing for in-depth internal structure analysis. Both types are crucial in research, industrial applications, and nanotechnology, driving innovations across various scientific fields.</p></p>
