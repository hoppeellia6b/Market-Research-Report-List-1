<p><p>The Congenital Heart Disease Occluder Market includes devices designed to close specific heart defects such as Patent Ductus Arteriosus (PDA), Atrial Septal Defects (ASD), and Ventricular Septal Defects (VSD). PDA occluders address a persistent opening between the aorta and pulmonary artery, ASD occluders close holes between heart atria, and VSD occluders seal openings between the ventricles. Each type of occluder plays a critical role in restoring normal blood flow and improving patient outcomes in congenital heart conditions.</p></p>
