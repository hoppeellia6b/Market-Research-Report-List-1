<p><p>The baking enzymes market comprises three main types: carbohydrases, proteases, and lipases. Carbohydrases enhance dough texture and fermentation, improving bread quality and shelf life. Proteases contribute to gluten strength, aiding in dough handling and softness. Lipases assist in fat breakdown, enhancing flavor and extending freshness. These enzymes are crucial in commercial baking, promoting efficiency and consistency while reducing production costs. The demand for clean-label and health-oriented products further drives innovation in this market segment.</p></p>
