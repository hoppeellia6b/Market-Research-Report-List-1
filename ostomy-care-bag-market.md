<p><p>The ostomy care bag market is primarily categorized into one-piece and two-piece systems, along with skin barriers. A one-piece system combines the pouch and skin barrier into a single unit, offering simplicity and ease of use. In contrast, a two-piece system separates the pouch from the skin barrier, allowing for more flexibility in changing pouches while maintaining the same skin barrier. Skin barriers, essential components in both systems, provide protection and adherence, ensuring comfort and minimizing skin irritation for patients.</p></p>
