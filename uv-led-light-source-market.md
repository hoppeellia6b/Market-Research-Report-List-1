<p><strong>The UV-LED Light Source Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Irradiation</li><li>Spot Irradiation</li><li>Surface Irradiation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV-LED light source market is segmented into three main types: linear irradiation, spot irradiation, and surface irradiation. Linear irradiation involves the use of long, continuous UV-LED arrays for applications like curing and printing, providing uniform exposure along a length. Spot irradiation focuses a beam of UV light on a specific area, ideal for targeted applications such as adhesive bonding or surface disinfection. Surface irradiation distributes UV light evenly over a surface, suitable for larger areas like coatings or sanitizing surfaces effectively.</p></p>
