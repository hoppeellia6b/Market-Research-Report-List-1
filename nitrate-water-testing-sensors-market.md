<p><p>Nitrate water testing sensors are pivotal for monitoring water quality, with two primary methods: electrode method and UV absorbance. The electrode method utilizes ion-selective electrodes to detect nitrate ions through electrochemical responses, offering rapid and direct measurements. In contrast, UV absorbance relies on measuring the absorption of ultraviolet light by nitrate ions in water, allowing for precise concentration analysis. Both methods contribute significantly to environmental monitoring and water quality management, catering to various industrial and regulatory needs.</p></p>
