<p><p>The isolation chips market encompasses three main types: optocouplers, magnetic couplers, and capacitive couplers. Optocouplers utilize light to transfer signals between input and output, providing high-speed performance and isolation. Magnetic couplers use magnetic fields for signal transmission, offering robustness and resistance to electromagnetic interference. Capacitive couplers rely on capacitive coupling for signal isolation, known for their compact design and low power consumption. Each type serves distinct applications in telecommunications, industrial automation, and consumer electronics, enabling safe and reliable signal transfer.</p></p>
