<p><p>The Active Infrared Detector Market encompasses two primary types: Thermal and Quantum. Thermal type detectors operate by sensing changes in heat and are typically used for motion detection and safety applications, providing broad wavelength sensitivity. In contrast, Quantum type detectors utilize semiconductor materials and are sensitive to specific wavelengths, making them suitable for applications requiring high precision, such as spectroscopy and environmental monitoring. Both types are essential in security, automation, and various industrial applications, each offering unique advantages based on their operational principles.</p></p>
