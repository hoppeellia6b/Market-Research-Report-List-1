<p><p>The Transmission Oil Cooler market is primarily categorized into three types: Tube and Fin, Plate and Fin, and Stacked Plate. Tube and Fin coolers utilize a series of tubes surrounded by fins to enhance heat dissipation, making them a popular choice for varied applications. Plate and Fin coolers feature thin plates interleaved to create flow channels, offering compact efficiency. Stacked Plate coolers combine multiple plates for increased surface area, enhancing cooling performance and making them ideal for high-demand environments.</p></p>
