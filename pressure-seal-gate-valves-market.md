<p><strong>The Pressure Seal Gate Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Socket Weld Connections</li><li>Butt Weld Connections</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pressure Seal Gate Valves market features two primary connection types: Socket Weld and Butt Weld connections. Socket Weld connections involve joining piping at a right angle, allowing for a smooth internal flow and are suitable for smaller sizes and higher pressures. In contrast, Butt Weld connections join pipes end-to-end, providing robust structural integrity, ideal for high-pressure and high-temperature applications. Both connection types are essential for ensuring leak-free performance and durability in critical industrial environments.</p></p>
