<p><p>Row crop cultivators are essential agricultural equipment designed for tillage and weed control in row crops like corn and soybeans. The market is segmented into three main types: mounted, trailed, and semi-mounted cultivators. Mounted cultivators are attached directly to the tractor, providing greater maneuverability. Trailed cultivators are towed behind the tractor, allowing for larger working widths while reducing the load on the tractor. Semi-mounted cultivators combine features of both, offering stability and improved weight distribution for efficient operation.</p></p>
