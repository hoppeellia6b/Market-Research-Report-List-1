The 4:1 ratio is commonly used in broadband applications, allowing better impedance matching for different frequency ranges. The 8:1 ratio is suitable for specific high-frequency scenarios, optimizing performance in specialized systems. Other impedance ratios cater to niche requirements, enhancing the versatility and adaptability of transmission line transformers in diverse applications.</p></p>
