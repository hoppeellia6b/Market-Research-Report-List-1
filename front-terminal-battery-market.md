<p><p>The Front Terminal Battery market primarily comprises AGM (Absorbent Glass Mat) and GEL batteries, both designed for efficient space utilization and easy installation in tight spaces. AGM batteries feature a fiberglass mat that absorbs electrolyte, providing high discharge rates and maintenance-free operation. GEL batteries, on the other hand, contain a silica gel electrolyte, offering enhanced safety and deep-cycle capability. Both types are ideal for applications in telecommunications, renewable energy systems, and uninterruptible power supplies, driven by their reliability and longevity.</p></p>
