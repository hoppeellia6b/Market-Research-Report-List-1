<p><p>The food contact paper market is divided into two main types: non-poly-coated and poly-coated paper. Non-poly-coated paper is often made from natural fibers and is biodegradable, making it environmentally friendly but less resistant to moisture and grease. In contrast, poly-coated paper features a plastic coating that provides enhanced durability, moisture resistance, and grease barrier properties, making it ideal for various food applications. Both types serve distinct purposes in food packaging and presentation, catering to different consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
