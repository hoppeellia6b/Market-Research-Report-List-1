<p><p>The cautery pens market is divided into high temperature and low temperature cautery pens, each serving distinct medical purposes. High temperature cautery pens are designed for tissue ablation and coagulation, effectively sealing blood vessels and minimizing bleeding during surgical procedures. In contrast, low temperature cautery pens are utilized for delicate tasks, such as removing warts or other minor skin lesions, providing precision without significant thermal damage. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing surgical outcomes and patient safety.</p></p>
