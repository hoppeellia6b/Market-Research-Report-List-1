<p><p>The gate valves market features four primary types: Solid Wedge, Flexible Wedge, Split Wedge, and Parallel Disk. Solid Wedge valves are robust and typically used in high-pressure applications. Flexible Wedge valves offer better sealing capabilities and adjust to misalignments. Split Wedge valves feature two separate wedges, allowing for smoother operation and easier maintenance. Parallel Disk valves, known for their low-friction operation, are ideal for throttling and flow control. Each type serves specific industrial and operational needs.</p></p>
