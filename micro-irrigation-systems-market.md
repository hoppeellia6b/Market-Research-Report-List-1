<p><p>Micro irrigation systems comprise various types designed to optimize water use and improve efficiency in agriculture. Drip irrigation delivers water directly to plant roots through a network of tubes, minimizing evaporation and runoff. Micro sprinkler irrigation disperses water in a fine spray, covering small areas effectively. Bubbler irrigation emits water in larger droplets, ideal for trees and shrubs. Other systems may include capillary and subirrigation methods. Each type enhances targeted water delivery, promoting sustainable farming practices and resource conservation.</p></p>
