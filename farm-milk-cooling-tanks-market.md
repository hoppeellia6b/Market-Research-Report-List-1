<p><ul><li>Horizontal Closed Tank</li><li>Silo/ Vertical Closed Tank</li><li>Open Tank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Farm milk cooling tanks are crucial for preserving milk quality post-harvest. The Horizontal Closed Tank features a cylindrical design that allows for efficient cooling and easy access, often used in small to mid-sized farms. Silo or Vertical Closed Tanks maximize space and promote rapid cooling with their tall structures, ideal for larger operations. Open Tanks, while less common, facilitate quick temperature stabilization and are simpler in design, but may expose milk to contaminants. Each type serves distinct needs based on farm size and milk handling requirements.</p></p>
