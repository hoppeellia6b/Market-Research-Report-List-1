<p><ul><li>Double-Sided PCB</li><li>Multi-Layer PCB</li><li>Single-Sided PCB</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive printed circuit board (PCB) market encompasses three main types: single-sided, double-sided, and multi-layer PCBs. Single-sided PCBs feature components on one side, making them cost-effective for simple applications. Double-sided PCBs allow components on both sides, enhancing design complexity and functionality. Multi-layer PCBs, with several stacked layers, offer high-density interconnections and improved performance for advanced automotive systems, such as infotainment and advanced driver-assistance systems. Each type caters to different performance requirements in the automotive industry.</p></p>
