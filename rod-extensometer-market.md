<p><strong>The Rod Extensometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Point Rod Extensometer</li><li>Multipoint Rod Extensometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rod extensometer market is divided into two main types: single point rod extensometers and multipoint rod extensometers. Single point rod extensometers measure displacement at a specific location, making them suitable for localized monitoring in various applications, such as civil engineering and geotechnical studies. In contrast, multipoint rod extensometers monitor displacement at multiple points along a structure or site, providing comprehensive data on strain and deformation for more extensive analyses. Both types are essential for ensuring structural integrity and stability.</p></p>
