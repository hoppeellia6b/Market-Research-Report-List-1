<p><strong>The Snow Melting Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydronic</li><li>Electric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Snow melting systems are primarily categorized into two types: hydronic and electric. Hydronic systems utilize heated water circulated through pipes beneath the surface to melt snow and ice, offering energy efficiency and consistent heat distribution. Electric systems, on the other hand, use embedded cables or mats that generate heat directly to melt snow. Both systems are popular for residential and commercial applications, providing safety, convenience, and reducing the need for manual snow removal in cold climates.</p></p>
