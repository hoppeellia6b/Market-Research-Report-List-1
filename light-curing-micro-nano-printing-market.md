<p><strong>The Light-Curing Micro-Nano Printing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-Photon Polymerization (TPP)</li><li>Single-Photon Polymerization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Light-Curing Micro-Nano Printing market includes Two-Photon Polymerization (TPP) and Single-Photon Polymerization methods. TPP allows for high-resolution 3D structures by using near-infrared lasers to polymerize photosensitive materials at a nanoscale, enabling intricate micro-architectures. In contrast, Single-Photon Polymerization utilizes visible light for polymerization, typically resulting in faster printing speeds but lower resolution compared to TPP. Both techniques cater to sectors like biomedicine, electronics, and materials science, enhancing capabilities in microfabrication and functional material development.</p></p>
