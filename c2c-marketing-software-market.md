<p><p>C2C marketing software can be categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based solutions host applications on remote servers, allowing users to access them via the internet. This model offers scalability, automatic updates, and lower upfront costs. Conversely, on-premises software is installed locally on a user's hardware, providing greater control and customization but requiring higher upfront investment and maintenance efforts. Both models cater to different business needs and preferences in managing consumer-to-consumer marketing strategies.</p></p>
